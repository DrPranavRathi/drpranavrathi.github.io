==About Me== {{ShowGooglePowerpoint|id=0B5jWjFYiQdkfUkppYWdieWhkVlE|size=medium}}

I love designing and building things. It does not matter if I am building a complicated scientific instrument or a simple gadget, I enjoys equally. I have many years of experience with product development. I designed and built optical tweezers, laser scanning confocal microscopes, Confocal Raman microscopes, customized confocal microscopes and spectroscopy instruments for AFMs. I have extensive experience in designing optical, opto-mechanical and opto-electrical instrumentation. I am proficient with Lab VIEW, Zemax, Solid Works, Spice and Mat Lab.
